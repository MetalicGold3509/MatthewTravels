Amazing Chennai

Chennai (/ˈtʃɛnaɪ/ (About this soundlisten), Tamil:
[ˈtɕenːaɪ]; also known as Madras,[a] the official name
|until 1996) is the capital of the Indian state of Tamil
Nadu. Located on the Coromandel Coast of the Bay of Bengal,
it is one of the largest cultural, economic and educational
centres of south India.
